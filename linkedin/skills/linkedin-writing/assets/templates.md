# Templates

Use these as starting structures, then adapt to the idea and audience.

## Template 1: Professional Insight

- Hook
- What I noticed
- Why it matters
- Example
- Takeaway
- CTA

## Template 2: Story To Lesson

- Hook with tension
- Situation
- What changed
- Lesson
- Reader implication
- CTA

## Template 3: Announcement Post

- Hook
- What changed or is happening
- Why it matters
- What to do next
- CTA

## Template 4: Framework Post

- Hook
- Name the problem
- 3-5 principles or steps
- Common mistake
- Closing summary
- CTA

## Template 5: Event Promotion

- Hook around the attendee problem
- Why this event matters now
- What people will learn or experience
- Who should attend
- Clear registration CTA

## Template 6: Document Post (Carousel)

- Cover promise
- Problem frame
- 3-7 insight frames
- Common mistake frame
- Closing takeaway
- CTA frame

## Rewrite Pass

When improving an existing draft, revise in this order:

1. Hook
2. First two paragraphs
3. Redundant lines
4. Specificity
5. CTA
